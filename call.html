Immercom 2023

Workshop on Mobile Immersive Computing, Networking, and Systems to be held in conjunction with MobiCom 2023

xx, xx, xx Oct, 2023

Call for Papers

Immersive technologies such as virtual, augmented, and mixed reality (VR, AR, and MR), which are often referred to as a universal term - extended reality (XR), have enabled numerous appealing applications in education, training, entertainment, and healthcare. Emerging applications and systems enabled by AR/VR/MR, especially the burgeoning metaverse, naturally involve multiple geographically distributed users interacting and communicating over the Internet. Given its interdisciplinary nature, XR has been extensively investigated in computer graphics, computer vision, and human-computer interaction communities. Nonetheless, XR and the metaverse put more strain on the underlying networks and systems to deliver high-quality immersive content in real time and guarantee a satisfactory user experience, which is still underexplored in the mobile computing and networking community.

This Workshop aims to bring together researchers, engineers, practitioners, and policymakers from academia, industry, and government to discuss the latest research and identify future opportunities for mobile immersive computing, networking, and systems. It solicits high-quality original research and novel developments that promote innovations to advance mobile immersive content delivery, AR/VR/MR, and the metaverse. It also welcomes papers that summarize research challenges, survey existing solutions, and/or propose visionary solutions to ongoing issues in mobile XR and the metaverse.

Topics of Interest

Key research topics include but are not limited to:

  • Development/Deployment challenges of 5G and beyond for AR/VR/MR and the metaverse
  • Edge computing, digital twins, and IoT for AR/VR/MR and the metaverse
  • Efficient visual sensing for augmentation and visualization
  • Harassment detection and prevention in the metaverse
  • High-performance and scalable rendering for mobile graphics
  • Immersive content delivery for mobile devices
  • Machine learning, data science, and blockchains for AR/VR/MR and the metaverse
  • Algorithms, theories, tools, and testbeds for AR/VR/MR and the metaverse
  • Multi-user spatial synchronization and 6DoF pose prediction in AR/MR
  • Novel applications of AR/VR/MR in education, entertainment, healthcare, training, etc
  • Scalability and heterogeneity of multi-user XR and the metaverse
  • Security and privacy in AR/VR/MR and the metaverse
